Introducing Pre-Order Vintages

At Gvinico, we love getting creative for our customers and winemakers. The idea for our Pre-Order Vintages started when we got a request for wines from Dano Winery—a client favorite—that had completely sold out. After chatting with Dano, we decided to offer pre-orders for future vintages. Now, we’re thrilled to bring this idea to you!

Why Pre-Order?

Pre-ordering gives you the chance to secure bottles from specific vintages before they’re even bottled. It’s the perfect way to ensure you don’t miss out on limited editions or the most sought-after Georgian wines when they’re released.

How It Works

Here’s how you can get started with pre-ordering:

  • Pre-Order Availability: Once the harvest and fermentation are completed, and the wines are aging, we open up pre-orders. Look for the "Pre-Order" label to reserve your bottles.
  • Secure Your Allocation: Place your pre-order and guarantee your share of the vintage when it’s ready to ship. No more worrying about missing out on your favorite wines!
  • Estimated Ready Date: Each pre-order wine includes an estimated ready date, giving you an idea of when it will be bottled and shipped. We’ll keep you updated throughout the aging process.

Alcohol Content

Since the wines are still aging, the alcohol content is not finalized. We provide an estimated range based on previous vintages or current aging conditions, with the final percentage confirmed at bottling.

Flexible Pre-Order System

We’ve designed the pre-order system to be as flexible as possible:

  • Tailored Terms: Pricing, prepayment, and delivery terms are flexible and can be customized based on your needs. Agreements are made between you, Gvinico, and the winemaker.
  • Continuous Updates: We’ll keep you informed throughout the maturing process, so you’ll always know when to expect your bottles.
