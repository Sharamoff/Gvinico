Whу is Gvinico?

Gvinico is a platform dedicated to showcasing Georgian wines to the international B2B market. Our team, filled with wine enthusiasts and staunch supporters of Georgian winemaking, built this platform to bridge the gap between exceptional winemakers and global markets.

We're on a mission to spread the love for Georgian wines planetary. Our shared passion for wine and deep respect for Georgian viticulture drive us to connect winemakers with international buyers seamlessly.

If you're a winemaker making fantastic wines and dreaming of going worldwide, we are here to help you make it happen. Gvinico – your gateway to the global B2B wine market.

Grow Your Brand

Gvinico is more than just another wine platform – it's your stage to tell your story and the unique aspects of your winemaking process, share your winemaking journey, and connect with a global audience of wine enthusiasts and professionals.

You have a chance to increase your brand visibility and establish yourself as a respected winemaker within the industry through in-depth interviews and engaging articles. And detailed information about your wines, accompanied by stunning photos and professional tasting notes, will help you find the perfect audience for your unique offerings.

Find new markets

We are committed to fostering seamless partnerships between Georgian winemakers and international buyers.

Our platform features comprehensive and regularly updated information about your wines, including technical specifications, tasting notes, and production details. This level of transparency empowers buyers to make informed decisions and find the perfect wines for their needs.

Now you have a single platform to represent your wine, publish the current wine stock, inform about the latest promotions and special offers and collaborate with other winemakers on unique and exciting offerings. So, we promote your specials and limited editions to a wide audience of potential buyers, regardless of their location. Our platform enables you to reach wine lovers and buyers even in regions that are not typically serviced by large wine companies with Georgian wines in its collection.

Join Gvinico

And unlock the door to a world of opportunities for your Georgian wines. Together, we can enhance the global recognition of Georgian winemaking and share its unique flavors with discerning wine professionals and enthusiasts everywhere.
